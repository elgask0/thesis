# CLAUDE.md

`AGENTS.md` is the canonical instruction file for this repository.

**Before doing anything in this repo, read `AGENTS.md` in full.** Follow it.
If this file and `AGENTS.md` ever disagree, `AGENTS.md` wins.

## Non-negotiable guardrails (restated for visibility only)

1. The thesis scope is frozen: two RQs, electricity is the primary outcome,
   CO₂ is complementary, CPC is downstream of RQ1, SCM is supplementary,
   economic outcomes are not core. Do not silently change any of this.
2. No results exist. Write the proposal as a plan. Do not claim DiD, SCM,
   or CPC findings. Do not treat `T0` dates as final. Do not overstate
   electricity-panel readiness beyond Gansu.
3. `literature_master.md` is the only approved source for which papers go
   where. Do not cite anything outside it. Do not reopen literature search.
   Do not invent references.
4. Draft **one subsection at a time** in `thesis-proposal.tex`. Replace
   `[TO BE DRAFTED]` only. Leave the `%` comments in place.
5. Every prose task uses the `writing-style` skill. Do not introduce its
   blacklisted vocabulary. Do not add meta-commentary about style or
   skills in the output.
6. If anything is missing, ambiguous, or ungroundable, stop and ask the
   user. Do not fabricate.

For everything else — source-of-truth hierarchy, literature workflow,
section drafting order, LaTeX editing rules, citation conventions, Zotero
integration, and prohibited behaviors — see `AGENTS.md`.
