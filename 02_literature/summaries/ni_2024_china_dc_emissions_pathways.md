We find a marked increase in emissions across various Chinese provinces, largely driven by computing scale. While projections suggest that data-center emissions could reach 430 million tons by 2050 (three times greater than 2021 levels), such emissions could potentially be reduced to 11–29 million tons under the “net-zero emissions” scenario.
